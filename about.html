About

The project is to develop a bogging web application where just like most of the social media accounts people can post blogs but instead of recordings it will be more about reading and writing and dynamic pictures. If a person is blogging on a travelling, then they can post their journey or experiences and sharing the other details like the location of the place, also guiding people or learning from others experiences as well. If a person is more into making videos then they can but for now it will be hard for me to do so.

JournalBlogging

The aim of this platform is to let the people share their experiences on the places they have visited just like keeping a life journal. So that people across the world can take benefits from there as well and also to get to know the beautiful places hidden across the countryside.
